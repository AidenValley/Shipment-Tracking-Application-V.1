What to Identify?

Now, we can identify the BOL (Bill of Lading) number within the Master Bill of Lading.

Master Bill of Lading number is divided within the two structures

1. SCAC Code: Standard Carrier Alpha Code that represents as an identification number for carriers in the U.S. within FOUR Alphabetical letters such as (CMDU, HDMU, OOLU).

2. Numbers / Letters: Usually within 8 to 10 digits (possibly mixture of Alphabetical letters).
